Image Processing and Computer Vision (IPCV) Laboratory, E&ECE Dept., IIT Kharagpur

This is an active group in the Department of E&ECE, IIT Kharagpur. The main research areas include:

  • Statistical learning
  • Deep learning
  • Computer vision
  • Image processing
  • Imaging inverse problems
  • Optimization
