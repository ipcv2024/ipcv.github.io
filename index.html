Image Processing and Computer Vision (IPCV) Laboratory, Department of E&ECE, IIT Kharagpur

This is an active group in the Department of E&ECE, IIT Kharagpur. The main research areas include:

  • Statistical learning
  • Deep learning
  • Computer vision
  • Image processing
  • Imaging inverse problems
  • Optimization
  • Medical image analysis
  • Computational intelligence
  • Eye movement analysis
  • 3D vision

Information for Prospective PhD Students and Post-Docs

  • For Ph.D, go here: https://www.iitkgp.ac.in/phd-admission
    Choose Electronics and Electrical Communication Engineering (E&ECE) as the department. Among the PhD options, you can choose:
    (1) The "Ph.D with Institute Assistantship" option. One can apply for the enhanced Prime Minister's research fellowship in the first year. https://www.pmrf.in
    (2) The "Ph.D with Individual Fellowship" option. To avail this option you will have to secure the fellowship first.
    Fellowships examples:
    Prime Minister's research fellowship (direct entry channel) - https://www.pmrf.in/guidelines
    Inspire Fellowship under Assured Opportunity for Research Careers (AORC) - https://online-inspire.gov.in/Account/INSPIREProgramme
    UGC-NET (Junior Research Fellowship) - https://ugcnet.nta.nic.in/information-bulletin
    Qualities we look for: Strong interest & background in pattern recognition and machine learning related math foundations, and familiarity with deep learning frameworks (e.g. Pytorch).

  • For Post-Doc, please go here: https://www.iitkgp.ac.in/pdf and apply for the institute's fellowship.
    Otherwise, you can also consider individual post-doctoral funding from agencies such as SERB (https://serbonline.in/SERB/npdf).
    Qualities we look for: Expert in mathematical foundations of pattern recognition and machine learning, expert in deep learning implementation frameworks, and a few publications in high quality journals /conferences.
