Image Processing and Computer Vision (IPCV) Laboratory, Department of E&ECE, IIT Kharagpur

This is an active group in the Department of E&ECE, IIT Kharagpur. The main research areas include:

  • Statistical learning
  • Deep learning
  • Computer vision
  • Image processing
  • Imaging inverse problems
  • Optimization
  • Medical image analysis
  • Computational intelligence
  • Eye movement analysis
  • 3D vision
